International Partnerships

We help small and medium-sized U.S. companies enjoy the benefits of global expansion through trusted international partnerships and help international companies partner with successful U.S. companies.

We've helped many U.S. companies search for, select, and manage worldwide partners, including distributors, resellers, and end-users of U.S. products and services.

Washington Performance Consulting has also helped many international companies partner with U.S. companies, including companies headquartered in Japan, China, Korea, India, Vietnam, Saudi Arabia, Turkey, Mexico, Colombia, and Brazil. For example, we can assist your company in gaining licensed access to proven U.S. business models and unique intellectual properties. In addition to establishing international partnerships, we provide ongoing consulting and assistance to support optimal growth.

WPC partners with:

  1. U.S. small to medium-size companies to represent them globally.
  2. International companies to help them gain access to a unique intellectual property and proven business model of U.S. companies we represent.
  3. Distributors interested in promoting the U.S. products and services of our clients internationally.

We Offer A Complete Range of Expert Services to Our U.S. Clients, Supporting Their Global Expansion and Success.

  • We develop a program to search for and select global partners for U.S. companies.
  • Lead the search and selection process for your international partners
  • Assist in the development of a tool kit to search for international partners
  • Develop a unique offer model for your company to sign up international partners

  • Take charge of all marketing & promotion programs for potential global partners and end-user clients.
  • Periodically travel to target markets to meet potential partners
  • Attend local or international conferences to promote your international partnership program

Having international business partners and distributors will make your company:

  • Fit in with the cultural norms of doing business in a foreign country.
  • Enjoy faster times to market.
  • Expand with no or little financial investment or risks.
